[1] A fire turned a Swedish dance hall jammed with teen-age Halloween revelers into a deathtrap, killing 60 people and injuring 155. [2] The fire, at the facilities of the Macedonian Association local immigrant group, was the deadliest in modern Swedish history. [3] The fast-spreading fire completely gutted the building and left rescuers facing a hideous scene that local rescue service leader Lennart Olin likened to a gas chamber. [4] Police said the list of injured included Somalis and people from current and former Yugoslavia. [5] The fire destroyed the building, whose second floor, where the dance took place, was rented by the local Macedonian immigrant association in Sweden's second-largest city. [6] The Goteborg rescue services also brought city buses into service to help transport the injured.
