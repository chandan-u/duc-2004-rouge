[1] In Oct and Nov 1998, the US was pursuing Osama bin Laden and his associates as suspects in the Aug bombing of US embassies in Kenya and Tanzania. [2] The attacks killed 224 and injured 5,485. [3] The US had ignored a warning of the Kenya attack 9 months earlier. [4] Two men were arrested in Tanzania. [5] One was under arrest in Germany. [6] Two were in a New York City jail, virtually held incommunicado, awaiting trial. [7] Bin Laden and his military commander also were indicted. [8] Two $5 million rewards were offered. [9] Bin Laden was believed to be in Afghanistan where the Taliban government called him an honored guest and said the US presented no proof that he was a terrorist.
