[1] The Truth and Reconciliation Commission, which was established to look into the human rights violations committed during the long struggle against white rule, released its final report. [2] In what has been described as one of the most complete reports of its kind, the commission blames most of the atrocities on the former South African government. [3] The ANC also came under fire for committing some atrocities during the struggle. [4] Nelson Mandella's ex-wife, Winnie could be prosecuted for the part she played in such violations. [5] Missing from the report was De Klerk, who threatened to sue if he was mentioned in connection with the atrocities.
