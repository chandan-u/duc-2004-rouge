[1] IOC executive board member, Marc Hodler, alleged that agents from cities bidding to host Olympic Games offer IOC members bribes to favor their city. [2] The allegation was made public when Holder revealed the Salt Lake City 2002 Games organizers offered scholarship payments to IOC members' relatives. [3] The IOC is investigating the charges and further allegations of Olympic corruption in voting for the games of 1996, 1998 and 2000.
